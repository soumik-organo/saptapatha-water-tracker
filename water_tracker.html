📐 How Reference Values Are Derived — Antharam (Organo, Chevella) ▼ Show

All reference values come from actual measured data at Organo Antharam — 182 houses, 60 acres, Chevella taluk, Telangana. ⚠️ Important: Readings were captured at ~10% occupancy (~73 of 728 planned residents present at the time of measurement. LPCD and per-house figures below are normalised to full design capacity (728 persons) for planning purposes — multiply current actuals by ~10× to estimate full-occupancy demand. The 5-Year Projection tab handles this ramp-up automatically via the occupancy % slider.

Demand Benchmarks
Reference ValueAntharam FigureFormula / Source
LPCD 169.35 L/person/day = Annual domestic (4.5 Cr L, measured at 10% occupancy) ÷ (design population 728 × 365 days) = 4,50,00,000 ÷ 2,65,720 = 169.35 (planning basis). Includes drinking, cooking, bathing, cleaning & in-house garden.
Note: Actual per-resident rate at measurement = 4.5 Cr ÷ 73 persons ÷ 365 = 1,685 LPCD — high because fixed infrastructure (common areas, clubhouse, roads) is shared by fewer residents at low occupancy.
Domestic / House / Month 20,604 L/house/month = Annual domestic (4.5 Cr L, measured at 10% occupancy) ÷ (design 182 houses × 12 months) = 4,50,00,000 ÷ 2,184 = 20,604 L/house/month (planning basis). Cross-check: LPCD × 4 persons × 30 = 169.35 × 4 × 30 = 20,322 ≈ 20,604 ✓
At 10% occupancy: 4.5 Cr ÷ 18 occupied houses ÷ 12 = 2,08,333 L/occupied-house/month — reflects combined fixed + variable costs at low occupancy.
Irrigation / Acre / Month 2,38,095 L/acre/month = Annual irrigation (2 Cr L) ÷ (12 months × 7 acres) = 2,00,00,000 ÷ 84 = 2,38,095. Validated against actual water meter on irrigation line.
Dripper Crosscheck Total 16,66,667 L/month = Annual irrigation (2 Cr L) ÷ 12 months = 16,66,667 L/month avg. This is the metered actual; dripper count × flow × time gives the bottom-up cross-check.
Swimming Pool Top-up 5,26,000 L/month Actual measured pool water consumption — accounts for evaporation losses (~3–5 mm/day), splash & backwash. Pool volume × turnover rate.
Other Consumption 83,555 L/month Residual metered consumption not classified under domestic, irrigation, or pool — includes community spaces, club house, roads washing, fire-fighting top-up.
Supply Benchmarks
Reference ValueAntharam FigureFormula / Source
RWH Storage per House 65,934 L/house = Total RWH storage capacity ÷ total houses = 1,20,00,000 L ÷ 182 houses. Used to scale project RWH storage proportionally.
RWH Total Storage 1,20,00,000 L (1.2 Cr L) Physical capacity of the Dugwell / sump at Antharam — engineered & constructed storage volume. Sets the ceiling on usable monthly RWH (each month's harvest is capped at this value).
RWH Monthly Supply (avg usable) 76,23,000 L/month = Σ min(monthly harvest, 1.2 Cr storage) ÷ 12. Total theoretical annual rainfall = 18 Cr L. Monthly harvest distributed by Chevella rainfall pattern — capped at 1.2 Cr L for Jun–Oct (monsoon months exceed tank capacity). Annual usable = 9.15 Cr L → monthly avg = 76.23 lakh L.
STP Monthly Supply 16,50,000 L/month = STP installed capacity × recovery rate × 30 days = 65,000 L/day × 85% × 30 = 16,57,500 L/month. STP treats greywater & blackwater; recovered water used for irrigation only.
Municipal / External Monthly 18,00,000 L/month = Municipal / tanker inflow × 30 = 60,000 L/day × 30 days. Allocated to Domestic supply only. Represents the gap-fill where on-site sources are insufficient.
Rainfall Data Source
ParameterValueSource
Monthly Rainfall (mm) Mar 13 → Jun 80 → Aug 179 → Dec 25 Long-term monthly averages for Chevella taluk, Rangareddy district from IMD (India Meteorological Department) historical records. Monsoon (Jun–Sep) contributes ~74% of annual 812 mm.
Seasonal Demand Factors DOM: ×0.85–1.2  |  IRR: ×0.5–1.3 Domestic factor peaks Mar–Jun (summer heat → ×1.2) and dips Jul–Sep (cooler monsoon → ×0.85). Irrigation factor peaks Apr–May (dry sowing → ×1.3) and drops Jul–Sep (rain-fed, minimal irrigation → ×0.5).
